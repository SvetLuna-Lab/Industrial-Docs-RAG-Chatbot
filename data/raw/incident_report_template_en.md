# Technical Incident Report Template

The purpose of this document is to define a unified structure for incident descriptions  
to enable further analysis, categorization, and RAG-system training.

---

## 1. General Information

- Date and time of the incident: _______________
- Site / location: ___________________
- Unit / equipment: _________________
- Name of responsible shift personnel: ___________________

## 2. Brief Incident Description

In 2–3 sentences, describe:

- what happened;
- where exactly it happened;
- what consequences it caused or could have caused.

Example:

> During scheduled transfer at PS-1, vibration level of pump unit N-250  
> exceeded the permissible threshold. An emergency shutdown of the unit was performed.

## 3. Technical Parameters at the Time of Incident

Fill in if data is available:

- Suction pressure: ________ MPa
- Discharge pressure: ________ MPa
- Bearing temperature: ________ °C
- Motor current: ________ A
- Vibration sensor reading: ________ mm/s

## 4. Personnel Actions

Describe the sequence of actions:

1. ____________________________
2. ____________________________
3. ____________________________

Additionally specify:

- Were emergency shutdown procedures executed?
- Were additional teams involved (maintenance, safety, HSE)?

## 5. Preliminary Cause Analysis

Provide a brief analysis of possible causes:

- technical factors (wear, defect, regulation violation);
- external factors (power surges, failures in related systems);
- human factor (wrong action, oversight).

## 6. Mitigation Measures and Recommendations

1. Measures taken to eliminate the consequences of the incident.
2. Recommendations to prevent recurrence:
   - adjustment of regulations;
   - additional personnel training;
   - changes in setpoints, algorithms, or procedures.

## 7. Attachments

- Log excerpts;
- Log files (if available);
- Photos / diagrams.
